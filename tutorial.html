Tagging Photos Tutorial

The application is really simple. It loads a photo from Flickr and asks you this question: Tag all the elements that you recognise

.

You then hover the mouse over tha elements that you identify, and draw a box clicking in the image, and you will be able to type the name of that element and save it.

You can do it as many times as you want. Simple, right? Now let's try it!

To find out how the project is progressing, to suggest changes to this application or to propose new research ideas based what you have tagged, please have a look at our community forum.
